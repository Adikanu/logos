SVG Logos

A collection of svg vector logos for everybody.

View All

The logos are optimized (removed duplicated paths / excessive groups / empty defs, linting, etc). Some were recreated from rastered images.

Tips for working with SVGs

• Always convert borders to paths. They use a fixed size and behave strangely when resized. • Join all the paths into one and use it as a background/mask. Or at least overlap your paths.
